## Common Opening Sentences

**Source:** 本報告書依循 GRI 通用準則 2021 編製。
**Translation:** This report is prepared in accordance with GRI Universal Standards 2021.

**Source:** 本公司致力於推動永續發展。
**Translation (audit-grade):** The Company is committed to advancing sustainable development.
**Translation (avoid):** ❌ The Company is fully and deeply committed to sustainability.

**Source:** 報告期間為 2024 年 1 月 1 日至 12 月 31 日。
**Translation:** The reporting period covers January 1 to December 31, 2024.

## Performance Statements (Past Tense)

**Source:** 2024 年，本公司溫室氣體排放量為 1,234.56 公噸 CO₂e。
**Translation:** In 2024, the Company's greenhouse gas emissions totaled 1,234.56 tCO₂e.

**Source:** 本公司於 2024 年完成 ISO 14064-1 查證。
**Translation:** The Company completed ISO 14064-1 verification in 2024.

## Policy Statements (Present Tense)

**Source:** 本公司實施反貪腐政策。
**Translation:** The Company implements an anti-corruption policy.

**Source:** 本公司每年進行重大性分析。
**Translation:** The Company conducts materiality assessment annually.

## Future Goals (Aims To / Will)

**Source:** 本公司預計於 2030 年達成淨零排放。
**Translation (preferred — softer commitment):** The Company aims to achieve net-zero emissions by 2030.
**Translation (acceptable — stronger):** The Company will achieve net-zero emissions by 2030.
**Note:** Use "aims to" by default for future targets to limit legal exposure unless source uses 必達/承諾 language.

## Common Canva Errors and Fixes

**Source:** 透過供應商評估降低供應鏈風險。
**❌ Canva (literal):** Through supplier assessment to reduce supply chain risk.
**✅ Corrected:** The Company mitigates supply chain risks through supplier assessment.

**Source:** 以員工為本，打造幸福職場。
**❌ Canva:** Take employee as the foundation, create a happy workplace.
**✅ Corrected:** The Company prioritizes employee well-being and fosters a supportive workplace.

**Source:** 我們積極投入社會公益。
**❌ Canva:** We actively engage in social welfare.
**✅ Corrected:** The Company is engaged in community initiatives.
**Note:** Replace "we" with "the Company" in formal disclosure tone.

## Stakeholder Engagement Pattern

**Source:** 本公司透過問卷調查、訪談、座談會等方式與利害關係人進行溝通。
**Translation:** The Company engages with stakeholders through surveys, interviews, and roundtable discussions.

## Materiality Assessment Pattern

**Source:** 經評估後識別出 8 項重大主題。
**Translation:** Following the assessment, eight material topics were identified.
**Note:** Spell out single-digit numbers in prose (eight), use digits for tabular data (8).
